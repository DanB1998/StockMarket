A Good Portfolio

A good portfolio will diversify your investment over several low risk stocks. This ensures that even if one of your safe, low risk sticks falls significantly it does not give a significant loss of your investment. Portfolio's should include around 10-30 stocks to be effective in keeping risk low.

A great example of a well rounded portfolio. Well rounded portfolios have their investment spread over different low risk sectors to ensure in the unlikely event of one of these low risk stocks failing, a significant loss is not made.
